What happens next

  1. A case manager reviews the inquiry
  2. Relevant hospitals or specialties are identified
  3. The patient receives next-step guidance
  4. Detailed coordination starts only if needed

What patients usually want to know first

  • Which hospital is suitable for this condition
  • How soon an appointment may be possible
  • What records to prepare before travel
  • What the likely cost range looks like
