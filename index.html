Frequently asked questions

Can MediBridge help before I travel to China

Yes. Patients can start by understanding options, reviewing available information, and preparing the next steps before travel.

Do you work with one hospital only

No. A core value of MediBridge is helping patients compare options across multiple institutions.

Can you help with records and translation

Yes. The service can include medical record organization, bilingual communication, and interpretation support.

Is this only for Chinese speaking patients

No. The platform is intended for international patients and families, with bilingual presentation and support.
